## Why this memo

A decision memo is not a status memo. It names the option chosen, what was foregone, and the condition that would reopen the decision.

## The four required elements

1. Decision (one sentence in question form).
2. Decision body (person or group).
3. What is foregone.
4. Review condition.

## Worked example (excerpt, fictional)

> Decision: Should the team pilot an AI code review assistant on new PRs?
> Decision body: Platform Engineering Council.
> What is foregone: wider rollout; PR-metrics rewrite; CI integration.
> Review condition: pause if more than 1 false-pass on security-critical review, or <10% review-time reduction after 30 days.
