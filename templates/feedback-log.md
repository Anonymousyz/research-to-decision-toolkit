## Why this log

Feedback is the next research question. Without a log the same feedback arrives 12 times from different channels and you keep answering it ad hoc.

## How to use

- One entry per feedback signal.
- Tag each entry with the channel.
- Add implication for next research question.
- After 5 entries, run an interpretation pass.

## Worked example (fictional entry)

## What this log is not

Not a customer-success log. Not a support ticket list. One specific tool: the next-research-question book.
